What is Animocha Studio?

Animocha Studio is a powerful 2D animation software that is designed for animating TV shows/animated series', animated movies, shorts, and more!


Powerful 2D animation software, now with collaboration!

Animocha Studio's killer feature is the ability to collaborate with other animators or artists within the same project!


Unique project formats & extra brewed goodness

Animocha Studio can save your projects as ".mocha" files, perfect for directly sharing with trusted friends! You can also marvelously export your masterpieces as your standard mainstream formats (.mp4, png, apng, gif, etc.).


Free, open-source, and transparent
